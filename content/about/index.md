Street photographer based in Cluj-Napoca, Romania. Shooting with a Fujifilm X100VI and whatever available light gives me.

I studied photography at university a long time ago, then spent a decade in design. This is me coming back to it — no agenda, just looking.

Everything here is shot on the street, unposed, usually within walking distance of home. I'm interested in the ordinary — the geometry of commuters, the texture of construction sites, the way a city organizes itself when nobody's watching.

There's a ten-minute window around 7:15 when the light cuts diagonally across Piața Unirii and everyone is moving with purpose. Nobody's lingering. The whole square becomes a machine.

{{</* img "001.jpg" "Crossing toward Strada Napoca, 7:12 AM" */>}}

{{</* img "002.jpg" "Bus queue stretching past the fountain" */>}}

I stood in the same spot for forty minutes. Nobody noticed me, which is exactly the point. The camera is small enough that people's eyes just slide past you.

{{</* img "003.jpg" */>}}

{{</* img "004.jpg" "The coffee vendor sets up at exactly 6:50 every morning" */>}}

What I keep coming back to is how the city choreographs itself without anyone conducting. Hundreds of people adjusting their pace to avoid each other, all pretending they're alone.
